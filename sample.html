Our Heritage

A Legacy Cast
in Steel

Established in the vibrant city of Rajahmundry in 1962, Rajahmundry Steel began as a modest enterprise with a single mission: to supply the finest quality steel products to the builders shaping Andhra Pradesh. Over six decades, that mission has never wavered.

Today, as a third-generation family business, we are proud to serve thousands of contractors, developers, homeowners, and industrial clients across East Godavari and beyond. Our warehouse stocks an exhaustive range of ISI-certified steel products backed by the most trusted brands in India.

What We Offer

Premium Steel
Products

From foundational TMT bars to precision stainless steel, our inventory covers every steel requirement across construction, fabrication, and industry.

01
TMT Bars

Thermo-Mechanically Treated reinforcement bars with superior ductility and earthquake resistance for all RCC structures.

02
MS Angles & Channels

Mild steel structural sections used in frameworks, fabrication yards, industrial sheds, and infrastructure projects.

03
Steel Pipes & Tubes

ERW and seamless steel pipes for plumbing, water infrastructure, structural columns, and industrial pipelines.

04
HR / CR Steel Sheets

Hot-rolled and cold-rolled sheets in multiple gauges for fabrication, roofing, press parts, and automotive use.

05
MS Plates & Flats

Heavy-duty mild steel plates for shipbuilding, bridge construction, pressure vessels, and machine bases.

06
Stainless Steel

Grade 304 & 316 stainless sheets, rods, and coils for pharmaceutical, food processing, and architectural applications.

07
GI & Colour Sheets

Galvanized and colour-coated corrugated roofing sheets offering weather protection for all building types.

08
Wire Mesh & Rods

Welded wire mesh, binding wire, and ribbed rods for concrete reinforcement, fencing, and ground stabilization.
